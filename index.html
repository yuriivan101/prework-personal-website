About Me

Hi, I’m Yuriy Ivanenko, and I am currently working as a Financial Analyst in our Corporate Finance department. I started with Power 3 years ago during Covid. My former employer was slowly going out of business, and I had to jump ship to survive.

I didn’t know anything about this company except that it was rated as a top place to work in the Philadelphia area. My former company was a small construction business and didn’t have the best owner. I really wanted to see what a top-rated company was like, and I have been very pleased with what I have discovered about Power and the culture here.

Since I started here, I have picked up coding as a problem-solving skill for myself. I have had many opportunities to apply that skill and help my team to be faster and more efficient. This has saved every individual hours per week.

I’m a very hands-on individual who likes to do things on his own if possible. This has manifested itself most prominently with my house DIY projects. My wife and I bought a fixer-upper house right before Covid. We ended up doing most of the work ourselves, including remodeling the kitchen and bathrooms. YouTube has been a great help to me over the years with most housework.

The most important thing that I have learned from coding and various construction projects is that I can learn and do almost anything.
